The "Plant" Constraint

Washington law (RCW 48.29.020) requires a "complete set of tract indexes." You must either build one or pay an existing plant for access. This is your biggest "non-negotiable" cost.

Individual Licensure: The Exam

Unlike CA, where a "Manager" just needs experience, Washington requires the "Designated Responsible Licensed Person" (DRLP) to pass a state-administered exam.

Exam Type WA Title Agent / Escrow Officer
Pass Rate ~65% (First Attempt)

Note: You must also provide fingerprints for a background check via IdentoGO, which takes about 2 weeks to clear.

Residency Rule

Washington is slightly more lenient on out-of-state residency than California, but you must have a physical office in WA or be in a reciprocal state with a "Non-Resident" license.
